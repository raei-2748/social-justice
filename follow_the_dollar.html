How every dollar is allocated

62.8 cents of every dollar
reaches the mission.

In FY2024-25, the Leukaemia Foundation raised $29.2M and spent $10.86M on fundraising and operations. That means 37.2 cents raised each dollar. The remaining 62.8 cents went directly to patient support, research, and advocacy. This is a significant improvement from 53.5 cents the year before.
